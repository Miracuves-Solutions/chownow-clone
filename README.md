## How It Works

1. Customer opens the restaurant branded app or website
2. Customer browses the menu, customizes items, and places an order
3. Order is sent directly to the restaurant POS and kitchen display
4. Restaurant accepts, prepares, and marks the order ready
5. Customer picks up or delivery driver collects and delivers
6. Payment is processed; restaurant keeps 100% of the revenue
